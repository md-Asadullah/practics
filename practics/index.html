HOW IT WORKS

Our user-friendly platform makes
currency exchange effortless

Sign Up

Create your account on FlutterLinks and
verify your identity for added security

Browse Marketplace

Explore available exchange listings to
find the perfect match for your currency
needs

Initiate Trade

Start a trade with another user. The
escrow system will hold the currencies
until both sides confirm the exchange

Complete Trade

Once both parties confirm the
exchange, the currencies are released
from escrow. Transaction complete!
